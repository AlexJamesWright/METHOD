Detailed Description

Two fluid self similar current sheet.

See Palenzuela et al 2009, Beyond ideal MHD: Towards a more... for explanation. Set up is one dimensional (x-direction, although may be run in multiple Ds), in equilibrium, with initial By field given by the error function. Behaviour should be diffusive for moderate resistivities. See Amano 2016 for two fluid details.
